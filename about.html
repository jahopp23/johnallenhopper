About

John has always been interested in computer software and media, such as photography and video. He studied the dichotomy of technology and human society while getting a degree in Anthropology from University of North Texas (2014). His college education proved to John that software engineerning and web development is one of the modern day's pinnacle activities, capable of creating a new digital culture and wonderful new ideas.

John wanted to pursue web development for himself, so he decided to receive professional training from Coding Dojo (www.codingdojo.com) in Dallas, Texas, where he currently resides. During his time at Coding Dojo, John gained the modern tools necessary for the creation of web applications, mobile-responsive sites, the proper approach to designing software, and how to transform brilliant ideas into code.

John then went on to work as a Front-End Web Developer for an award-winning advertising agency in Dallas - Johnson & Sekin, where he tandemly developed two premiere client websites, mexene.com and teasdalelatinfoods.com. John has created numerous projects with a wide array of code and frameworks, as well as developing projects daily. Now, John seeks a position as Front-End Web Developer, UI Developer, or UI/UX role. John's passion for visual aesthetics, design, engineerning, and the web render him a highly-capable and versatile developer.

John has received the highest certification from Coding Dojo in Ruby On Rails - Black Belt

In his free time, John enjoys reading on philosophy, anthropology, politics, and technology. He lives in Dallas, Texas.
